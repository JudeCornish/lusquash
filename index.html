About our club

We are the Lancaster University Squash Club! Whoever you are and whatever your ability, come and have a go at this unique sport. We encourage all to join us at our regular socials, meals and trips, so come along and get to know fellow squash players - everyone is invited!

We currently boast:

  • 2 Mens BUCS teams
  • 1 Womens BUCS team
  • 1 Division 3 Regional team
  • 1 Division 5 Regional team
  • 1 Mens Development Squad
  • 1 Womens Development Squad

We are active on Facebook and Instagram and would love you to follow our journey. You can follow us @LUSquash.

Fundraising

During these difficult times, the Lancaster University Squash Club believes that they have a responsibility to make a difference to the lives of those put at risk by the outbreak of COVID-19, and want to do something to help.

Trying to match this desire to help with the aim of maintaining squad fitness over this quarantine period, LU Squash will be undertaking a special charity fitness challenge, with all proceeds going to the Cumbria COVID-19 Response Fund, in which any and all of its squad members can participate and contribute.

The aim is simple: to collectively run 2020 kilometers as a squad by June 15th 2020 at 23:59. Some may run more than others, but like this pandemic, we are all in this together. Please give generously to the cause, and keep up with us on our Strava page to see how close we are to the target!
