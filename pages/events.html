[01/02/2020]

Independence Themed Social

A thoroughly planned, challenge based social where teams competed under the theme of Brexit

Planned to fall on the night of Brexit, the independence social was one of competitive challenges between six teams:

Team May - led by Abbey Scott

Team Farage - led by Ed Park

Team Corbyn - led by Alex Vahey

Team Cameron - led by Louis Fisher

Team Mogg - led by Jacob Vahey

Team Johnson - led by Josh McCurdie

Louis Fisher's team Cameron came out victorious after 14 grueling (mainly) drinking related challenges. Although with a different theme, this is a social we certainly look to replicate in the future. The night ended with heading off to sugar to dance the night away.

[29/01/2020]

Bangor away

A successful away trip to Bangor University that turned into a Beach day with the boys

The BUCS second team, consisting of Captain, Ed Park, Jude Cornish and Jacob Vahey dominated an away game in Bangor and asked the coach driver whether it would be possible to stop off at the coast on the way back in celebration. To their surprise he agreed and they sprinted off to the beach for a quick, freezing cold dip in the sea.

Special mention also has to go to Ed Park for making the tactical move of hitting his opponent... down there...

What made this even more of a power move was that as his opponent weeped on the floor from the heavy blow, Ed took the opportunity to practice his forehand drives in preparation for when his opponent was ready to continue.

[17/01/2020]

England National Squash Centre

Alumni trip to the England National Squash Centre in Manchester

We were lucky enough to be able to take a team trip to the England National Squash Centre. We played on their beautiful full glass courts, something many members of the society had never had the chance to experience. We also got to play on their specialised doubles courts that are extended in width.

We hope to keep this traditional trip running year on year as it was and always has been, such a great experience for the society and members at all levels of squash.

We were joined by some old boys of the club who got to meet the new freshers and catch up with old teammates.
